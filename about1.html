My story

I came from Karenni minority community. Despite limited education, my parents encouraged me to study, and to try my best. In 2021, I studied at Karenni High School, gaining leadership and community manage skills. After earning there, I continued studying at Deeku Education Center, then improved academic skill. In 2024, I returned to teach English at Karenni High School, where I learned patience, familyship, and overcame challenges with human resources.
